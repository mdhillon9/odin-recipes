Red Velvet Cupcake Recipe

Delicious Red Velvet Cupcakes

prep time: 15 minutes

cook time: 20 minutes

Yummy and fluffy cupcakes with a dollop of cream cheese on top, make for a decadent dessert that's super easy to make and super delicious!

Cream cheese frosting gives it a mild sweetness with some tang, to give that extra special something to your red velvet cupcakes

Ingredients

Cupcakes:

  • 1 2/3 cups all purpose flour, 200 g
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder, 5g
  • 3/4 cup powdered sugar, 175g
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda, 1g
  • 1 tsp baking powder, 4g
  • 2 Tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt, 1g
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, 170 g
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • red food coloring (optional)

Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 3 sticks unsalted butter at room temp, 340g
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 lb confectioners sugar sifted, 550g
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 16 oz cream cheese, 455g

Instructions

Cupcakes:

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, sift the dry ingredients together (including the sugar). Give it a whisk to combine.
  3. Cut the room temperate butter into small pieces and mix into the dry mixture
  4. In another bowl, whisk together the eggs, buttermilk, food coloring, and vanilla extract
  5. Slowly add the wet ingredients together to the dry ingredients until combined
  6. Distribute the batter evenly into cupcake papers
  7. Bake at 350 degrees F for 15-20 minutes
  8. Once complete, stick a toothpick from the top of one cupcake. It should come out clean if it's done!

Frosting:

  1. Cream the butter and sift in the confectioners sugar into a large bowl
  2. To that, add room temperature cream cheese, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Mix or beat together
  3. Add confectioners sugar one cup at a time.
  4. Transfer the frosting to a piping bag and pipe large dollops to each cupcake.
