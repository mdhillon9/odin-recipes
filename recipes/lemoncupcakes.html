Lemon Cupcake Recipe

Delicious Lemon Cupcakes

prep time: 20 minutes

cook time: 20 minutes

These easy to bake lemon cupcakes are tangy and fluffy with decadent lemon buttercream frosting!

A lovely lemon buttercream frosting gives it a mild sweetness with some tang, to give that extra special something to your lemon cupcakes

Ingredients

Cupcakes:

  • 1 2/3 cups all purpose flour, 200 g
  • 1 cup powdered sugar, 175g
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda, 1g
  • 2 Tbsp lemon zest
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder, 4g
  • 2 Tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt, 1g
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, 170 g
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 1/4 cup sour cream, 120 ml
  • 1/4 cup whole milk, 60ml
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice, 60ml

Buttercream Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter at room temp, 226g
  • 4 cups powdered sugar, 540 g
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp lemon zest

Instructions

Cupcakes:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Add the sugar and lemon zest together and pulse in a food processor.
  3. In a large bowl, sift the dry ingredients together (excluding the sugar). Give it a whisk to combine.
  4. In another bowl, whisk together the eggs, sugar, milk, melted butter, lemon juice, sour cream.
  5. Slowly add the wet ingredients together to the dry ingredients until combined.
  6. Distribute the batter evenly into cupcake papers.
  7. Bake at 375 degrees F for 15-20 minutes.
  8. Once complete, stick a toothpick from the top of one cupcake. It should come out clean if it's done!

Frosting:

  1. Cream the butter, salt and lemon zest, and sift in the powdered sugar into a large bowl.
  2. To that, add in the lemon juice.
  3. Transfer the frosting to a piping bag and pipe large dollops to each cupcake as preferred.
